# Consent and Capacity Specification

## Purpose

The app must handle self-completion, assisted completion, and proxy completion with care. It cannot assess legal capacity, but it must collect enough context to make the completion pathway transparent.

## Completion Mode

Before questions begin, ask:

Who is completing this?

1. I am completing this for myself.
2. I am helping someone complete this.
3. I am completing this on behalf of someone who cannot complete it themselves.

## Required Fields by Mode

### Self Completion

Capture:

- resident name
- acknowledgement that answers may be used to generate printable documents

### Assisted Completion

Capture:

- helper name
- helper relationship to resident
- acknowledgement that the resident is participating as much as they are able

### Proxy Completion

Capture:

- proxy name
- proxy relationship to resident
- acknowledgement that answers are based on the proxy's knowledge of the resident
- acknowledgement that sensitive or uncertain information should be treated cautiously

## Consent Page Text Requirements

The consent text must explain:

- all questions are optional
- answers can be edited
- answers can be excluded from printed outputs
- outputs may be seen by staff and visitors if printed and placed in the room
- the booklet is not truly private if kept at bedside
- private family record content is excluded from printed outputs

## Visibility Warning

Before output generation, show:

> Please review each answer carefully. Posters may be seen by anyone entering the room. A bedside booklet may be read by staff, visitors or others in the room. Mark anything sensitive as private if you do not want it printed.
