# Care Passport — Clinical Boundaries

## Product Boundary

Care Passport is a personal identity and support-preferences tool.

It is designed to help carers, support workers, healthcare providers, family members and others understand the person they are supporting.

It is not a clinical care plan, clinical record, medical assessment, treatment plan, risk assessment, behaviour support plan, medication record, or professional recommendation.

## Broader Support Context

Care Passport may be used in aged care, disability support, healthcare, community support, rehabilitation, respite, palliative care, or other support environments.

The same principles apply in all contexts.

The product provides personal information, preferences, routines and identity information.

It does not provide clinical instructions, treatment plans, risk assessments, behavioural management plans, medication advice or professional recommendations.

## Output Disclaimer

Generated outputs should include language substantially similar to:

> This document is intended to help people understand the person behind the care or support needs. It does not replace clinical assessment, support planning, professional judgement, or the policies and procedures of any service provider.

## Language Rules

Use supportive, person-centred language.

Preferred phrases:

- helpful things to know
- preferences and routines
- what helps me feel comfortable
- information that may help people understand me
- helpful things to know when supporting me

Avoid directive phrases:

- care plan
- instructions for staff
- staff must
- required care
- care directives
- mandatory support requirements

## MVP Rule

The MVP must not generate text that appears to direct, instruct, override or replace professional judgement.
